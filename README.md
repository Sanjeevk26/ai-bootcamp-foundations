# AI for Everyone Bootcamp
## From Fundamentals to Generative AI & Responsible AI

Welcome to the AI for Everyone Bootcamp.

This repository is designed to take you from zero background in AI to a strong conceptual understanding of:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Machine Learning
- Deep Learning
- Generative AI
- Generative AI Tools
- Responsible AI & Ethics

No heavy math required to start.
No coding background required in the beginning.
Just curiosity.

---

# Who Is This For?

This bootcamp is designed for:

- Students
- Working professionals
- Business leaders
- Non-technical learners
- Anyone curious about AI

Whether you want to understand AI conceptually, apply it in business, or transition into the AI field — this roadmap will help you.

---

# What You Will Learn

This repository covers five major pillars:

---

## 1️⃣ AI Foundations

You will learn:

- What Artificial Intelligence actually is
- Narrow AI vs General AI
- Real-world applications of AI
- Why AI is booming now
- Industry adoption trends
- AI in daily life

Goal: Build strong conceptual clarity.

---

## 2️⃣ Machine Learning

You will understand:

- What is Machine Learning?
- Supervised vs Unsupervised Learning
- Classification & Regression
- Training vs Testing
- Overfitting & Underfitting
- Bias & Variance
- Evaluation Metrics (Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1)
- Real-world ML examples

Goal: Understand how machines "learn from data".

---

## 3️⃣ Deep Learning

You will explore:

- Neural Networks
- Activation Functions
- Hidden Layers
- Backpropagation (conceptually explained)
- CNNs (Computer Vision)
- RNNs & Transformers (NLP basics)
- Why Deep Learning changed AI

Goal: Understand how modern AI models work.

---

## 4️⃣ Generative AI

You will learn:

- What is Generative AI?
- How LLMs (Large Language Models) work
- Tokens & Embeddings
- Transformers (high-level explanation)
- Diffusion Models (for images)
- Text-to-Image models
- Prompt Engineering basics
- Limitations of Generative AI

Goal: Understand how ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc. work.

---

## 5️⃣ Generative AI Tools

Hands-on exploration of tools like:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Midjourney
- DALL·E
- Copilot
- AI coding assistants
- AI productivity tools

You will learn:

- Use cases
- Strengths & weaknesses
- Practical workflows
- How businesses leverage these tools

Goal: Move from theory to application.

---

## 6️⃣ Responsible AI & Ethics

You will explore:

- AI bias
- Fairness in models
- Privacy concerns
- Deepfakes
- Surveillance risks
- Job displacement
- Hallucinations
- Model transparency
- Governance frameworks
- Human-in-the-loop systems

Goal: Understand the risks and responsibilities of AI.

---
